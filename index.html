The Importance of a Good Water Filtration System

What Is a Water Filtration System?


A water filtration system is a device that is installed in your home to remove impurities from your tap water. It does this by using various filters to trap contaminants, remove them from the water, and ensure that the water supply from your tap is clean and safe to drink.

Importance of Quality Water Filters


There are many different types of water filtration systems available on the market, so it is important to do your research and find one that is right for your home. Below, we discuss the benefits that a good water filtration system can bring:

Reduces the Risk of Contamination


One of the main benefits of a house water filter is that it can reduce your risk of contracting waterborne illnesses and diseases. These diseases are caused by harmful microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, parasites, and chemicals, which can be found in unfiltered water. If you drink tap water containing these contaminants, you may develop diarrhea, vomiting, and even more severe health problems. By investing in a quality water filtration system, you can be sure that your family is protected from these waterborne diseases.

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) investigates waterborne illness outbreaks in public water systems. The CDC collects data on the reported cases of waterborne illness and compiles it into an annual report. The report includes information on the causes of the outbreak, the number of people affected, what groups are most likely to be affected, and the steps taken to control the outbreak. Below, we provide a quick summary of the CDC report.

According to their data, some of the top causes of outbreaks in public water systems include:

  • Stormwater
  • Sewer overflows
  • Wildlife
  • Fertilizers, pesticides, or other chemicals
  • Manufacturing operations
  • Cracks in water pipes or other issues involving the distribution system
  • and more.

According to their data, these germs and chemicals are some of the most common contaminants:

  • Norovirus
  • E. coli 0157
  • Giardia
  • Hepatitis A virus
  • Salmonella
  • Arsenic
  • Copper
  • Lead
  • Nitrate
  • Radon
  • and more.

Groups with a higher risk of being affected:

  • Infants
  • Young children
  • Pregnant individuals
  • Older adults
  • Individuals with weakened immune systems

Improves the Taste of Your Water

If you have ever noticed that your tap water has a strange taste or odor, it is likely due to the presence of contaminants. Water filtration systems will remove these contaminants, leaving you with clean, fresh-tasting water. This means that you will be able to enjoy the authentic taste of your drinking water, without any strange aftertastes.

Prevents Bad Odors In the Water


As well as improving the taste of your drinking water, filtration systems will also prevent foul odors. This is because it removes contaminants such as lead and other metals, which can cause your water to smell bad.

Better For the Environment


Not only is filtered water better for your health, but it is also better for the environment. This is because, unlike bottled water, filtering your own water at home reduces the amount of plastic you need. Bottled water requires a significant amount of energy and resources to produce, so by filtering your own water, you can help to reduce the plastic bottles in landfills and, consequently your carbon footprint.

Cost-Effective

Another importance of water filtration is that it is cost-effective in the long run. This is because you will no longer need to purchase bottled water, which can add up pretty quickly.

Better Quality of Life


Overall, a water filtration system can improve your quality of life. This is because it will provide you with clean, fresh-tasting water that is free from contaminants. It will also reduce your risk of contracting waterborne illnesses/diseases and can save you money in the long run. Investing in a quality water filtration system is an essential step in ensuring that you and your family have a safe, healthy, and enjoyable life.
